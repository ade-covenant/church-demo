About Our School

We are one of the largest, most diverse school in the State with over 100 students in Otu, and a further 50 studying across 7 towns for First Baptist Model Schools and College. First Baptist Model Schools and College, Otu was established by the Church (First Baptist Church, Otu) in 1992 for public benefit and it is recommended globally. Our aim is just like our motto which is "Education For Brighter Future." We aim to use education pave a shiny way for our pupils.
