Baptismal Class

Embark on a journey of spiritual rebirth as we delve into the waters of baptism, guided by the teachings of Christ in our Baptismal Class.

More about the Baptismal Class

And Peter said to them, “Repent and be baptized every one of you in the name of Jesus Christ for the forgiveness of your sins, and you will receive the gift of the Holy Spirit. (Acts 2:38) This is in following the footsteps of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ, the head of the church, who during His earthly sojourn went through water baptism by immersion. Therefore, as a true believer in Christ, you are expected to submit yourself to water Baptism by immersion. As a church, we also believe in a saved church membership, hence we strongly believe that for you to be a full/proper member of the church, you must first be saved and subsequently baptized by immersion.

To be baptized after being saved:

  • Join the Enquirer’s class Here
  • Complete the 6 lessons of the syllabus
  • Write the test at the end of the lessons
  • Upon satisfactory completion of enquirer’s class, you will be duly certified fit for Baptism
  • Baptismal services are conducted at different intervals, and you will be informed of the next available date
  • Upon baptism, you will be officially admitted as a member of the church.
Baptismal Class holds on Sunday during Sunday School (9:30-10:30), 9:00-10:00 am (when we have combined service)
